OUR VALUES

Cedar Park High School Esports is a student led organization the aims to provide students the opportunity to compete against other people and high schools in different video games while creating a diverse community of students who share the same passion.

GAMES

By partnering with organizations such as "High School Esports League", we are able to compete in a wide variety of games.

JOIN

  • Build your communication, teamwork, and leadership skills.

  • Earn college scholarships.

  • Practice critical thinking and problem solving.

  • Join a team of dedicated individuals focused on taking the competition to the next level.
